Notes on tolerance choices:
- CL and CD: 1e-5 relative (0.001%) is the ROADMAP requirement.
- CM: Use absolute tolerance since CM can be exactly 0 for symmetric airfoils at alpha=0. For non-zero CM, 1e-5 absolute is equivalent to ~0.02% for CM=-0.0515.
- CDF and CDP: CDF should match 1e-5 relative. CDP is by subtraction (CD - CDF) so errors compound; 1e-4 is reasonable.
- Transition x/c: 1e-3 (0.1% of chord) is appropriate since transition location depends on the BL solution.
